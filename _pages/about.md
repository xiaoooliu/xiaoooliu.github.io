# About Me
I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by [Prof. Yuguang Wang](https://yuguangwang.github.io/). My research interests include generative AI, diffusion models, flow-based generative models, reinforcement learning, and trustworthy AI systems. I am particularly interested in building powerful and reliable AI systems that can generalize beyond interpolation, respect physical and semantic constraints, and acquire knowledge through interaction and experience.

Prior to joining SJTU, I received an MRes in Connected Electronic and Photonic Systems from University College London and the University of Cambridge with Merit, and a BEng in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Nottingham Ningbo China and the University of Nottingham with First Class Honours. During my MRes study, I worked on deep learning and machine learning projects for biomedical sensing, supervised by [Prof. Manish Tiwari](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/41595-manish-tiwari), [Prof. Adrien Desjardins](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/29409-adrien-desjardins), [Prof. Andreas Demosthenous](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/10892-andreas-demosthenous), and [Prof. Dai Jiang](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/5102-dai-jiang). My undergraduate research was supervised by [Dr. Shun Bai](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shunbai/). I also worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Huawei 2012 Laboratories, focusing on machine learning algorithms for GNSS positioning and large-scale software system refactoring.
